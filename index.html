I created a logo is for a coffee shop and gallery:

The logo uses soft chocolate tones which mimic rich, warm coffee. Soft and comforting, this logo invites customers in for a unique experience as they sip, chat and browse. The cursive font keeps things welcoming and professional, while also maintaining an air of whimsy, which is essential for the artistic flare. The mouse is simply a whimsical character that pulls from my own personality and art style.
